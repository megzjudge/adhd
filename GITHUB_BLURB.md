## Longer blurb (for the README intro / social card / search snippet)

**Adult ADHD self-check with the DIVA-5.** This is a free, static, no-tracking website that
explains the **DIVA-5 (Diagnostic Interview for ADHD in Adults)** — the most in-depth adult ADHD
instrument in wide use — and helps you make sense of your results. Take the free online self-check,
then enter your **total score** and your **inattention / hyperactivity / impulsivity** averages to
see where you land against the screening threshold, with clear charts and plain-language guidance.

It also publishes a **complete worked example**: a real, fully filled DIVA-5 showing all 18 DSM-5
symptom criteria across both childhood and adulthood, the ticked examples, written notes, and
impairment across five areas of life — the depth a quick ADHD quiz never reaches.

No responses are collected. The self-check is a screening aid, **not a diagnosis** — a full
assessment requires a qualified clinician.
